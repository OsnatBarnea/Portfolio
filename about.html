About me

My journey to software engineering demonstrates my ability to master complex subjects and
adapt to new challenges. With a PhD in Earth Science focused on active faults and earthquakes,
I developed strong analytical skills and attention to detail through rigorous scientific research.
My experience in seismic engineering honed my problem-solving abilities and taught me to work with
mission-critical systems where precision is paramount. Seeking greater opportunities for innovation,
I transitioned to full-stack web development, where I combine my analytical background with creative problem-solving.
My unique blend of scientific expertise and technical skills allows me to approach development challenges
with both systematic precision and creative solutions.
I'm passionate about creating robust, user-focused applications and am eager to contribute my diverse skill set
to challenging professional projects.
